Get to know me!

My main interests lie in UX/UI design and branding, but I'm also actively working on improving my skills in programming and 3D modelling, as well as video editing. I'd describe myself as a curious and multicultural person who loves to learn new things and take on challenges.

My aim is to create engaging and user-friendly digital experiences that not only look great but also function seamlessly across all devices. I believe in the power of design to solve problems and enhance user satisfaction.
